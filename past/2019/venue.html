Address

39106 Magdeburg
Germany

Directions

By plane

  • The nearest airports are Hanover (140 km), Leipzig (150 km), and Berlin (150 km).
  • From there, take the train to Magdeburg.

By train

  • With the Deutsche Bahn (DB), travel to the main station Magdeburg-Hauptbahnhof.
  • Due to extensive construction works at Damaschkeplatz and around the central railway station, take a 10 minutes walk from the main exit of the railway station to central tram station Alter Markt.
  • From tram station Alter Markt, take tram number 5 in direction Messegelände/Elbauenpark and get off at station Askanischer Platz (a 10 minutes ride).
  • From there, walk over the crossroad (Walther-Rathenau-Straße) towards Sandtorstraße where you will find the Max Planck Institute on the right side.
  • Current timetables are available at Magdeburger Verkehrsbetriebe.

By car

  • Leave the motorway (Autobahn) A2 at exit Magdeburg-Zentrum or A14 at exit Magdeburg-Sudenburg.
  • Turn onto B189/B71 (Magdeburger Ring) and leave at exit Zentrum/Universität, direction B1 (Burg/Dessau).
  • Follow the street signs towards B1 and go straight through the tunnel Universitätsplatz and continue on B1.
  • Turn left towards Rothensee/Hafen into Sandtorstraße at the traffic lights before crossing the bridges.
  • The Max Planck Institute is directly on the right side.
  • Parking space is available at the institute.
